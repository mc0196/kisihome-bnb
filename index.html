Where We Are

Vestreno, above Lake Como

A hidden gem in the Italian Lake District — where mountains meet water

Vestreno is a small, peaceful village perched on the hills above Dervio, on the eastern branch of Lake Como. From here, you can enjoy breathtaking views over the lake and easy access to all that the area has to offer.

  • 5 min to Dervio town centre & lake shore
  • 30 min to Lecco by car
  • 1 hr to Milan by train from Dervio
  • 15 min to Colico ferry terminal
